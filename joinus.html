About Us

InnovXplorers is a teen-run, non-profit organization! We provide high-schoolers a platform to team up with others, utilize their skills needed for nextgen job seekers and learners, and create curated portfolios and CVs for our participants.

Learn More

How We Work

Students who join InnovXplorers will be assigned a team where the teammates have similar skills and interests. Once the groups are finalized, these teammates will decide on a project, brainstorm a plan, and execute their venture!

Students will be given a deadline of ONE MONTH to create the project. While every member of the team will collaborate, the team leader will submit a report according to the protocol. Additionally, the team member's portfolio will be updated with their project links and scores (team management skills, critical thinking, etc.).

Projects will be uploaded in Git Hub Repository.
