Frequently Asked Questions

  • I don't have expert technical Knowledge. Will I be still able to participate?

    You can participate. We provide a platform for you to grow. If you have any technical queries, you can feel free to post it in the Forum for your primary skill.

  • I am good in Science but I don't have technical knowledge. Will I be still able to participate?

    You can join as a content writer. You have to chose Blogging as your primary skill in this case.

  • What if my leader writes a incorrect report about me?

    The report will be reviewed and verified by the directors before taking into consideration. As a member, you can view the reports submitted.

  • What if my team mate behaves in an inappropriate manner?

    You can report your team mate through the Form that will be provided to you. Use of abusive words, vulgar language and gender oriented comments will not be tolerated. The Member will be terminated in this case.
